# Weekly Review Template

**How to use**: Paste this into Claude with your answers filled in, or invoke `/weekly-review`
in Claude Code.

---

## Tell Claude This First

```
Run a weekly review for me using the format below.

This week:
- Finished: [list what you completed]
- Didn't finish: [list what you planned but didn't do]
- Surprised by: [anything unexpected, good or bad]
- Weighing on me: [what's unresolved going into next week]
- Top 3 priorities for next week: [list them]
```

---

## Output I Want

**Wins (this week)**
[List finished items as concrete achievements, not tasks.
Example: "Shipped the auth redesign" not "Worked on auth."]

**Gaps & Causes**
[For each unfinished item: one sentence on why it slipped. No editorializing — just the cause.]

**Patterns**
[2-3 observations about the week. Connect to my goals if you know them.]

**Next Week Plan**
[The three priorities as a ranked list. Under each: one "definition of done" sentence.]

---

## Optional Follow-Up

After Claude produces the review, you can ask:
- "Dig into [gap] — what specifically should I do differently?"
- "Help me plan [priority 1] in detail for Monday morning"
- "Draft a status update for my manager based on this review"
