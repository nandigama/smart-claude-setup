# /content-strategy

Builds a content strategy from the audience up — not a list of blog topics, but a system
that connects content to business outcomes with a repeatable production process.

## Step 1 — Gather Context

Use AskUserQuestion to collect:

1. What is your product and who is your target customer? (ICP description)
2. What is the business goal for content? (brand awareness / lead generation / SEO / customer education / all)
3. What content do you currently produce? (nothing / ad hoc / some blog posts / active program)
4. What channels are you targeting? (blog / LinkedIn / newsletter / YouTube / podcast / other)
5. What is your content team's capacity? (solo / small team / agency)
6. What does your buyer care about most? (their job to be done, not your product features)

Do not produce any output until the user answers all questions.

## Step 2 — Produce the Content Strategy

### Content Pillars

3-4 thematic pillars that anchor all content to the audience's interests AND the brand's expertise:

**Pillar 1: [Name]**
- Audience interest: [what they care about]
- Brand relevance: [why you have authority here]
- Content types that fit: [formats]
- Example topics: [3-4 specific titles]

**Pillar 2-4: ...**

**The test for a good pillar**: "Does a piece of content in this pillar make our target customer smarter at their job?" If yes, it belongs. If it only promotes the product, it doesn't.

### Content-to-Pipeline Mapping

Content mapped to buyer journey stage:

| Stage | Buyer's Question | Content Goal | Format | Example |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Awareness | "What is this problem?" | Educate / build trust | Blog, social, podcast | [specific title] |
| Consideration | "How do others solve this?" | Position solution | Case study, comparison | [specific title] |
| Decision | "Why you vs. alternatives?" | Differentiate | ROI calculator, demo | [specific title] |
| Retention | "How do I get more value?" | Expand / retain | Tutorial, webinar | [specific title] |

### Editorial Calendar (90 days)

| Week | Pillar | Format | Title / Angle | Channel | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | [pillar] | Blog | [specific title] | Blog + LinkedIn | [role] |
| 2 | [pillar] | Newsletter | [specific angle] | Email | [role] |

Produce 12 weeks of content with specific titles (not just topics).

### Channel Strategy

For each channel:
- **Goal**: what this channel is for in your mix
- **Content type**: what performs on this channel (not what you wish performed)
- **Posting cadence**: realistic for the team's capacity
- **Success metric**: how to know it's working

### Distribution > Creation

For every piece of content, plan the distribution:
- Primary post (the channel it's made for)
- Repurposed for: [2-3 derivative formats — e.g., blog → LinkedIn thread → newsletter section]
- Amplification: [internal sharing, community posting, outreach to quoted sources]

## Step 3 — Content Brief

Ask: "Do you want me to write a content brief for the first piece in the calendar — the specific angle, key points to cover, SEO target keyword, and call to action?"
